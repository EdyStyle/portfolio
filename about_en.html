03 : About

Know me a little bit better

I am a lifelong learner, self-taught individual, and I am fascinated by personal development and contributing to the growth of others. I enjoy living life and creating unique moments.
I cultivate the philosophy that we can change the world by making a difference in one person's life at a time!
That's why I have been working with people since my adolescence, inspiring, motivating, and teaching. I have traveled across Brazil and the world, getting to know different cultures (which is one of the main reasons I enjoy learning and speaking other languages), exploring arts, techniques, and embracing life. Above all, I strive to be my best self and contribute wholeheartedly!

Here's a glimpse into my professional journey

As a free spirit, I have worked in various fields, primarily as a freelancer. I have been a teacher, administrator, salesperson, retail operator, call center representative, and DJ. Throughout my journey, I have participated in numerous competitions where I experienced wins, losses, and valuable lessons. I have been involved in several projects focused on commercial growth, business expansion, and the development and leadership of high-performing individuals and teams. These experiences have taken me across markets in Europe, Latin America, and Brazil. Embracing a diverse range of roles and markets has allowed me to gain a broad perspective and adaptability. It has shaped my ability to connect with people from different backgrounds, understand their needs, and find innovative solutions. I am constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to expand my horizons. I believe in the power of continuous learning and personal growth, and I am committed to making a positive impact wherever I go. Please explore my portfolio to delve deeper into the specific projects, achievements, and skills that have shaped my professional journey. Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about my experiences!"

Regarding priority

I have a passion for promoting an inspiring culture driven by a people-first mindset, with the ultimate goal of providing a world-class experience for employees and customers. I am dedicated to investing in personal growth, as well as the growth of others, always operating with integrity and making the most of the journey!
